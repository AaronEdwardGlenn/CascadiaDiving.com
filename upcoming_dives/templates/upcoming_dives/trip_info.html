Here is a list of everything you will need to bring on your trip with Cascadia Dive Club:

Dive Gear

  1. CERTIFICATION CARD
  2. BCD
  3. REGULATOR
  4. TANK(s)
  5. MASK/SNORKEL/FINS
  6. WETSUIT
  7. HOOD/GLOVES/BOOTIES
  8. DIVE COMPUTER
  9. MASK DEFOGGER
  10. WEIGHTS ON WEIGHT BELT
  11. DIVE KNIFE
  12. FLASHLIGHT

Peronsonal Gear

  1. PARKING PASS (if needed)
  2. TOWEL

  7. DIVE LOG
  8. FIRST AID KIT
  9. OUTDOOR SHOES
  10. JACKET
  11. PHOTO ID

  1. ANGELING/SHELLFISH LICENCE
  2. SPEARGUN
  3. EXTRA SPEAR
  4. EXTRA RUBBERS
  5. CRAB BAG
  6. FISH STRINGER
  7. CRAB GAUGE
